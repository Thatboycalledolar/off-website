About Raven’s Crown Care

At Raven’s Crown Care, we are dedicated to providing care for adults living with developmental disabilities. Our priority is to create a comfortable and safe living environment where our residents can thrive. We take pride in our ability to provide not only a place to live, but a sense of community and camaraderie as well. Our highly trained staff is always available to offer help when needed, but we also encourage our residents to maintain their independence and live fulfilling lives.

Our Services

We offer various services to ensure comfort and hospitality

Personal care

Assistance with hygiene, medication administration, toileting, dressing, eating, and drinking

House Management

Multiple house management services including meal preparation, cleaning, organizational skills etc

Communication Skills

Identifying choices, expressing wants and needs, thoughts, expressive and receptive language skills, interacting in social settings

Academic Skills

Budgeting, time management, calendar skills, number concepts

Community Skills

Navigating the individual’s community settings, Recreational skills such as formal and informal supports, clubs such as Special Olympics, church activities. Etc

Behavioural Management

Utilizing coping strategies, maintaining baseline behavior, teaching, modeling, prompting, and reinforcement of positive behaviors, supervisionManagement of symptoms of mental illness
